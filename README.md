### 1. Market Dashboard (`MySpareParts_Market_Dashboard.html`)

Interactive HTML dashboard showing:
- **Market Overview**: TAM, growth projections, competitive landscape
- **Competitor Analysis**: 25+ players across 4 categories
  - Startups: Partao, SPARETECH, Partium, Synthavo, Ovoko
  - Distributors: TVH Group, Kramp, AGCO Parts
  - OEMs: CNH, Caterpillar, John Deere, Liebherr, JCB
  - Marketplaces: Mascus, IronPlanet, Ritchie Bros
- **Funding Landscape**: Recent investment rounds
- **Technology Battleground**: AI/ML adoption across players
- **Strategic Recommendations**

**To view:** Open `MySpareParts_Market_Dashboard.html` in any web browser.

### 2. N8N Newsletter Workflow (`n8n-workflow-v2.json`)

Automated weekly intelligence newsletter that runs every **Monday 8AM CET**.

**Features:**
- OEM stock tracking (Deere, CAT, CNH, AGCO, US Steel)
- RSS news aggregation from 12+ sources
- Competitor monitoring (Partao, SPARETECH, Partium, Synthavo, Ovoko, TVH)
- AI-powered executive summary via Perplexity
- Professional HTML email with MySpareParts branding

**Sections:**
- Executive Summary (AI-generated)
- Competitor Watch
- Industry News
- Agricultural Parts
- Construction Parts
- Technology & AI
- Funding & Deals
- EU Regulatory
- OEM Updates
- Supply Chain

**To use:** Import `n8n-workflow-v2.json` into your N8N instance.
