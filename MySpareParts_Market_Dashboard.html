OEMs are digitalizing fast but want reach. Kramp-John Deere partnership model. Could complement OEM platforms rather than compete.

5

Raise to Match Competition

Partao: €3M, targeting next round. Partium: $15M. Ovoko: €20M. To compete at scale, need capital for tech + ops + expansion.

Regulatory Tailwinds 2025-2026

⚖️ Right to Repair Directive
Reinforces parts availability, repair-first policy. Legitimizes independent aftermarket.
🌱 ESPR (Ecodesign)
Circularity requirements. Better parts metadata mandated. Helps cataloguing.
📊 Data Act (Sep 2025)
Fair data access for connected products. Enables predictive maintenance data flows.
🔒 Digital Services Act
Marketplace governance. Trader traceability. Compliance is barrier to entry.
🛡️ Cyber Resilience Act
Security for IoT/vehicle connectivity. Affects platform architecture.
🔍 CSDD (Due Diligence)
Supply chain ESG requirements. Marketplace can productize compliance.
